Evidence Labelling

The purpose of this annotation task is to label whether evidence from a Wikipedia page is suitable to fully support or refute simple factoid sentences called claims.

Annotation guidelines and advice are available in our ArXiv paper: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1803.05355.pdf. Please read the appendix sections A.7.1, A.7.3 and A.8. Section A.7.2 does not apply to this task.

The consent form you agreed to is available here: https://fever.shef.ac.uk/consent.
